Race the 100m, 200m, or 400m against AI competitors or friends. Adjust difficulty and pacing. Ideal for sharpening your speed and learning the rhythm.

Live the life of a track athlete — from tryouts to college recruiting battles. Handle burnout, school pressure, and tough coaching decisions in a deeply immersive simulation.

🏔️ Distance Mode (Coming Soon)

Push your limits in 800m, mile, 2-mile, and cross country events. Fatigue carries over between heats and meets. Master pacing and mental strength.

💡 AI & Genetics

Every athlete has hidden traits — some are fast starters, others develop top-end speed. Genetics, training, and mental toughness all matter.

🌎 Multiplayer + Rivals

Play online with up to 8 others using race codes. Rivals from your past races show up in future meets. Everyone's career continues — even when you're not playing.

📅 Career + Championship Season

Progress through your high school season — conference, regionals, state, and nationals. Manage energy, confidence, and school pressure while chasing glory.

🔥 Next-Gen Sprint Mechanics

Feel every step. Our rhythm-based sprint system uses timing, momentum, and biomechanics — no more button mashing. Get false-started and you're out. Perfect start? You're gone.

📈 Hidden Potential System

Not every star shines right away. Some athletes develop late, others peak early. You'll never know your true ceiling — only how hard you're willing to train.

🧠 Mental Game

Confidence and pressure affect every race. Your performance changes depending on your mindset, recent results, rivalries, and crowd energy.

🏆 College Recruiting & NIL Battles

Compete for offers from fictional schools with real reputations. Choose between chill training programs or cutthroat 'sink or swim' environments — but choose wisely.

👥 Community & Customization

Every race can be saved, shared, and replayed. Track your stats, unlock new gear, and show off your growth from day one to the podium.
